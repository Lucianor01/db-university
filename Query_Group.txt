1. Contare i corsi raggruppati per cfu

    1. SELECT `cfu`, COUNT(`id`) FROM `courses` GROUP BY `cfu`;

    2. SELECT `date_of_birth`, COUNT(`id`) FROM `students` GROUP BY `date_of_birth`;
